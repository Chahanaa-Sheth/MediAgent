Migraine is a neurological condition that can cause intense headaches, nausea, and sensitivity to light.

Fever may occur because of viral infections, bacterial infections, or inflammatory conditions.

Period pain may happen due to uterine contractions, hormonal imbalance, endometriosis, or fibroids.

Dehydration can cause headaches, dizziness, fatigue, and dry mouth.

Coughing may happen because of throat infections, allergies, flu, or respiratory diseases.
